English source: {source_text}
Systems (name: translation):
{service_blocks}
Decision protocol:
- Read the source carefully; identify named entities, terminology, and ambiguous constructions.
- Compare each translation against the source for adequacy, fluency, terminology accuracy, and register match, and assign per-service quality scores.
